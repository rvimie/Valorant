TACTICAL FIELDS

Each map serves as an open stage to showcase your strategic innovation. Tailor your positioning for local combat zones.

ABYSS

A hidden subterranean fortress in Norway built around a lethal, bottomless pit. Featuring zero outer boundaries, a single misstep sends players falling to their death across its vertical, three-lane layout.

BIND

Located in Morocco, Bind features two Spike sites with a unique twist: there is no middle area. Instead, it relies on two one-way teleporters that allow teams to execute rapid, sound-heavy rotations and surprise flanks across the desert facility.

BREEZE

Located in a remote island in the southern Bermuda Triangle, Breeze is a wide-open beachside resort featuring massive sightlines, large open chokepoints, and mechanical trap doors that demand long-range aim duels.

CORRODE

An eco-terrorist and toxicologist from Australia, Corrode deploys corrosive chemical clouds and melting acid pools that slowly dissolve enemy armor and degrade their vision, decaying anyone caught in his zone.

HAVEN

Located in Thimphu, Bhutan, Haven features a unique three-site layout instead of the usual two. This massive monastery grounds forces defenders to spread their resources thin across A, B, and C sites while giving attackers plenty of room for mind games and split pushes.

PEARL

Located in a dome underwater in Lisbon, Portugal, Pearl is a straightforward, traditional three-lane map with no gimmicks like teleporters or ropes, forcing pure aim duels and strategic pathing through its tight corridors and long B-ramp.

SPLIT

Located in Tokyo, Japan, Split features a massive central tower split by two distinct high-ground areas. It relies heavily on vertical ziplines and ropes, forcing teams to fight for mid control to efficiently rotate between two highly defensible, enclosed Spike sites.

ASCENT

Located in Venice, Italy, Ascent features an open middle area that serves as a playground for diverse utility usage. It relies heavily on thick, mechanical breakable doors on both Spike sites that players can close or destroy to manipulate rotation paths and site control.

FRACTURE

Located in New Mexico, USA, Fracture features an H-shaped layout that splits the map in two, allowing attackers to pinch defenders by spawning on both sides of the map. It relies heavily on long ziplines that run directly underneath the central defender spawn.

ICEBOX

Located in an arctic excavation site in Russia, Icebox features a freezing climate dominated by shipping containers and snow-covered cranes. It relies heavily on verticality, ziplines, and complex high-ground angles that reward sharp aim and aggressive aerial plays.

LOTUS

Located in the Western Ghats of India, Lotus is a three-site map set in a lost, ancient city of stone and flora. It relies heavily on unique mechanics like rotating stone doors, a breakable wall, and a silent drop chute, requiring fast rotations across its interconnected lanes.

SUNSET

Located in Los Angeles, USA, Sunset features a traditional three-lane layout with a heavy focus on mid control. It relies on a mechanical breakable door and tight, urban alleyways that force close-quarters brawls and fast-paced utility play.
